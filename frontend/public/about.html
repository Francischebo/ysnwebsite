About Youth Synergy Network

Discover our story, vision, and the passionate team driving youth empowerment across Kenya.

Our Identity: Who We Are

Youth Synergy Network (YSN) is a national youth-driven organization that exists to empower, mobilize, and mentor young people in Kenya to become changemakers in their communities, campuses, and careers. Rooted in legal activism, civic engagement, social justice, and sustainable development, YSN is a cross-disciplinary platform that brings together university students, high school leaders, community youth, and professionals under one synergized banner of transformation.

Founded in 2022 by two visionary twin brothers, Omwansa Onduko and Kemosi Omwansa, YSN was born from the realization that Kenyan youth, despite their potential, remain underrepresented in policy dialogue, access to legal resources, mental health support, and opportunities for leadership. YSN fills this gap by fostering inclusive spaces for learning, action, advocacy, and mentorship.

Our Founders & Leaders

Meet the passionate team driving our mission forward.

Omwansa Ombati

Patron

A senior mentor and professional consultant, commissioner Ombati provides guidance on organizational structure, partnerships, and long-term impact strategy. His wisdom and grounding in social development lend YSN its stability and institutional vision.

Omwansa Onduko

Founder

A law student, youth rights advocate, and visionary leader passionate about justice, social equity, and mentorship. Omwansa has led numerous campaigns on legal education, sanitary pad dignity, and civic participation in schools and universities.

Kemosi Omwansa

Co-Founder

Kemosi complements the vision with his deep interest in climate justice, mental health awareness, and the role of law in development. As a co-strategist and active mentor, he has inspired dozens of students to become agents of change.
