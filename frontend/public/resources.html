University of Nairobi

UoN Synergy Chapter

Est. 2021
150+ Members

The flagship chapter with a strong focus on constitutional literacy and environmental law advocacy. Their "Law in Simple Language" program has reached over 5,000 students across faculties.

Key Initiatives:

  • Weekly legal literacy clinics
  • Campus tree planting campaign
  • Mental health peer counseling
  • High school mentorship program

Kenyatta University

KU Synergy Leaders

Est. 2022
120+ Members

Key Initiatives:

  • Monthly sanitary pad drives

Start a Chapter at Your University

Bringing the Youth Synergy Network to your campus in four simple steps

1

Form a Core Team

Gather 5-10 passionate students from diverse faculties with interest in legal literacy, climate action, period dignity, or mental wellness.

2

Secure Faculty Advisor

Identify and engage a supportive faculty member, preferably from law, environmental studies, psychology, or gender studies.

3

Apply Online

Complete our chapter application form, submit your action plan, and schedule an orientation call with the national team.

4

Launch Your Chapter

Receive your starter kit, training resources, and official certification to begin your campus impact journey.
